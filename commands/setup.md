# /setup

Connect your documents. Three questions. Every newsletter gets smarter after this.

---

**1. What's your Google Drive folder for this brand?**

The folder where your brand guide, audience personas, past newsletters, and performance data live. Exact folder name or path.

*Example: "See Monterey" or "Clients/See Monterey/Marketing"*

---

**2. Which email platform are you using?**

a. Beehiiv
b. Mailchimp
c. ConvertKit / Kit
d. Klaviyo
e. Other (name it)
f. None yet — I'll copy the output manually

---

**3. How many audience segments do you actively email?**

This sizes the segment variants in each `/create` run.

*Example: "3 — newsletter subscribers, past customers, leads" or "1 — everyone gets the same email"*

---

After you answer, I'll connect to your folder, read what's there, and show you a one-line summary of what I found — including how many images are available and whether learning files already exist.

If something important is missing from your folder, I'll tell you what it is and how it affects output — but I won't block you from creating newsletters.

---

**Folder structure that works best:**

```
Your Brand Folder/
├── brand-guide.md         (or .pdf, .docx — any format)
├── audience-personas.md
├── past-newsletters/      ← examples of what you've sent before
├── images/                ← newsletter images, headshots, product shots
└── performance/           ← open rates, click rates, any data you have
```

You don't need all of these. More documents = better output. One brand guide is enough to start.

---

*This is optional. `/create` works without setup — you can paste brand context, describe your audience, or provide examples directly. Setup just means you don't have to repeat yourself every session.*
